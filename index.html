About Myself

Dr. Radhesh R Menon

MBBS, MS Orthopaedics (Gold Medal), Fellow of Hand & Microsurgery - Japan, Fellow of Arthroscopy & Sports Medicine, FIFA Diploma in Football & Sports Medicine

Dr. Radhesh R Menon, is a gold medallist orthopaedic surgeon specializing in hand surgery, microsurgery, wrist & shoulder arthroscopy, and sports medicine. With advanced training from Japan he is an expert in minimally invasive techniques, Dr. Radhesh is dedicated to providing precise diagnoses and effective treatments for trauma, sports injuries, and upper limb conditions.
