About Myself

Dr. Radhesh R Menon

MBBS, MS Orthopaedics (Gold Medal), Fellow of Hand & Microsurgery - Japan, Fellow of Arthroscopy & Sports Medicine, FIFA Diploma in Football & Sports Medicine

Dr. Radhesh R Menon is a well-disciplined orthopaedic surgeon dedicated to patient care and well-being. Dr. Radhesh completed his MBBS degree with first-class results from Vydehi Institute of Medical Sciences, Bangalore in 2017. Driven by his passion for orthopaedic surgery, he pursued postgraduate training at the prestigious K S Hegde Medical Academy, Mangalore. His dedication and skill earned him the Prof. Dr. B Jayaprakash Shetty Endowment Gold Medal, recognizing him as the best out-going student among the candidates in the batch of 2017. Dr. Radhesh has presented his thesis at both international and national conferences, showcasing his commitment to academic excellence. He has also participated in numerous advanced courses to stay at the forefront of orthopaedic advancements. During his Senior Residency at Kanachur Medical College, Mangalore, he played a pivotal role in organizing the “SHOULDER CONCALVE 2022”, demonstrating his leadership and commitment to continuous education in orthopaedics. Dr. Radhesh, is also a trained surgeon in Arthroscopy & Sports Medicine under Dr. Nataraj H M, a leading knee and shoulder surgeon in Bangalore. Dr. Radhesh has a keen interest in Hand & Microsurgery, this led him to undertake advanced training at the esteemed Saitama Hand & Microsurgery Institute, Japan. There, he had the privilege of learning from world-renowned surgeons, gaining expertise in hand, wrist and elbow surgeries, trauma reconstruction, and congenital deformity corrections.

Dr. Radhesh is respected by his peers and mentors, he combines a wealth of surgical expertise with a patient-centered approach. He is committed to providing state-of-the-art care in Orthopaedics, hand surgery, sports surgery, and microsurgery, ensuring the best outcomes for his patients.
